About Roots

About Roots Industries Private Limited

Roots Industries Private Limited, founded in 1970 and headquartered in Coimbatore, India, is a globally recognized leader
in manufacturing automotive and industrial components. With a strong commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability, Roots has consistently delivered world-class products and services to customers across the globe.

Our Journey & Expansion

Over the decades, the company has expanded into multiple sectors, including automotive horns, electrical components,
industrial cleaning systems, and precision engineering. Roots is renowned for its robust R&D capabilities, advanced manufacturing practices, and commitment to international standards.

Divisions & Capabilities

Roots operates several specialized divisions such as:
Automotive Horns & Accessories , leaning Equipment Division , Precision Engineering Division , Electrical & Electronic Systems.


Global Presence & Recognition

Roots exports its products to over 60 countries and is a trusted OEM supplier to major brands.
With certifications like ISO and IATF, the company stands for engineering excellence and global standards.
